In memory of Eric Sean Webber

Founder of Team Infinity

Eric Sean Webber saw technology as a way to extend life and liberate human beings from drudgery. His vision for the future, the manifestation of which he considered inevitable, he called 'RoboEco': a fully automated society which allowed humans to live free from the constraints of labor and limited resources. Eric coined the term Team Infinity to encourage us to constantly expand our expectations of what is possible.
